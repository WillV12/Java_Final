Chapter I

Long ago, in a forgotten place beneath moonlit skies, a tale began that no quill had dared to ink. The winds whispered of old magic and solemn vows bound in silence and gold.

Chapter II

The candlelight flickered as if it too remembered. Dust stirred like ancient breath, and every shadow leaned closer, listening. His hand trembled as it hovered above the book, afraid to turn the next page.
